Contact TSA Tracker

Use this page for data issues, airport requests, partnerships, or general questions about TSA Tracker.

General Inquiries

For questions about the site, feedback on wait-time accuracy, or general comments, email benbirkhahn10@gmail.com. Typical response time is 1–2 business days.

Reporting Inaccurate Data

If a wait time looks wrong or appears stuck, send the airport code, the time you noticed it, and what you observed on site. That gives us enough detail to check the source feed and catch upstream issues faster.

Airport Data Requests

If you know of an airport that publishes live TSA wait times publicly, or you work at an airport and want to discuss a data partnership, send the airport code and any source links you know about.

Advertising and Sponsorships

For advertising or sponsored placements, email the address above with the subject line "Advertising." We review each inquiry directly.

Privacy and Data Requests

For privacy or personal-data questions, review the Privacy Policy first. If you still need help, email with the subject line "Privacy Request."

Press and Media
